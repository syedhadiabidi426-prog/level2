Snapchat

Featuring a ghost symbol, this messaging app is known for ephemeral media, allowing users to send photos and videos that vanish shortly after being viewed.

Facebook

Recognized by a white lowercase "f" on a blue background, this platform is a global networking site used to connect with friends and family, share photos and updates, and join communities.

X(Tiwtter)

Represented by a minimalist black or white stylized "X", this platform is a microblogging service used for real-time news, quick public conversations, and sharing short updates.

Tiktok

Shown as a stylized musical note, this app is heavily focused on short-form, creative, and highly viral video content, especially trending dances and music.

Instagram

Featuring a stylized gradient camera icon, this highly visual platform is dedicated to sharing photos, short videos (Reels), and stories with followers.

Youtube

Identified by a white right-pointing triangle inside a red, rounded rectangle, this video-hosting giant allows users to watch, share, and upload a massive variety of video content.
